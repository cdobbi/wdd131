Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to offer others the opportunity of adopting a Rare German Rhinelander Rabbit. We want to share the joy of raising and caring for these beautiful creatures with ohters.

Audience

The primary target audience for the Rhinelander Rabbitry Site includes individuals interested in purchasing rabbits as pets, emotional support, or for breeding purposes. This audience may consist of pet owners, breeders, and rabbit enthusiasts looking to learn more about the breed. The site aims to attract individuals who are passionate about rabbits and are seeking reliable information and resources to care for their bunnies or start a breeding program.

Scenario Questions and Answers

  1. Scenario Question: What sets you apart from other rabbit breeders?
    Answer: At the Rhinelander Rabbitry, we take pride in our commitment to breeding healthy, well-socialized rabbits with excellent temperaments and distinctive show-worthy coat markings. Our rabbits are raised in a well-kept and loving environment, receiving proper care and nutrition to ensure their well-being. We also provide ongoing support and guidance to adoptees to help ensure a successful transition and happy life for the bunnies.
  2. Scenario Question: How do I know if a Rhinelander rabbit is the right breed for me?
    Answer: Rhinelander rabbits are considered a sport breed and not a commercial or standard pet breed. They are social, intelligent, active exotic animals that require daily interaction, exercise, and mental stimulation. They are easily trained, enjoy being trained, pet and snuggled next to. Rhinelanders are the Border Collie's of the rabbit world. If you are looking for a loving and engaging pet that will bring joy to your life, a Rhinelander may be the perfect choice for you. They make us laugh and smile daily!
