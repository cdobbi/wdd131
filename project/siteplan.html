Site Domain: rhinelander-rabbitry.com

Site Name: Rhinelander Rabbitry

This name represents our rabbitry that is focused on breeding and caring for Rhinelander and Harlequin rabbits.

Purpose

The site provides a hub for rabbit enthusiasts by listing available rabbits for adoption, providing rabbit care information, and offering a contact form for inquiries.

Scenarios

  • What is the best way to care for a Rhinelander rabbit?
  • What are the costs associated with adopting a rabbit?
  • Do you have any show rabbits available for purchase?
  • What is the best diet for a Rhinelander rabbit?
  • Can a Rhinelander rabbit be an emotional support animal?

Color Scheme

The selected site colors are:

  • Primary Color: #b06834 (SaddleBrown) - Used for headings and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #FFD700 (Gold) - Used for background and highlights.

Typography

The selected fonts are:

  • Heading Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif - Used for all headings.
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - Used for all body text.
